Technical Overview

We combine cutting-edge custom drone hardware with advanced AI and satellite connectivity to enable autonomous, intelligent drones that collaborate naturally with people, turning complex tasks into simple conversations.

Custom Drone Hardware

Built on a TBS Source One V5 frame with a SpeedyBee F405 V4 stack, our drones feature high-quality carbon fiber construction, advanced flight controllers, and powerful ESCs capable of delivering up to 55A continuous current per motor.

AI & Robotics

We leverage state-of-the-art artificial intelligence to give the drone real-time perception and decision-making abilities. Our fine-tuned Pi0 robotic foundation model serves as the "brain" of the UAV, handling functions such as object recognition, obstacle avoidance, and high-level navigation decisions autonomously.

Starlink Connectivity

Integrated Starlink satellite internet connectivity provides a high-bandwidth, low-latency data link between the drone and remote compute resources virtually anywhere on the globe, enabling real-time streaming of HD video and sensor data.

Real-Time Decision Making

With the combination of onboard AI and Starlink connectivity, our drone achieves real-time decision-making capabilities that enable on-the-fly target recognition, dynamic rerouting, and adaptive mission parameters based on live data.

Future Vision: Drones as Natural Language AI Agents

Our vision is for drones to become intelligent agents that humans can interact with as naturally as we do with AI assistants. We foresee a future in which deploying and tasking a drone is as simple as issuing a voice or text command, and the drone understands the intent and carries it out autonomously.

Natural Language Tasking

Instead of programming flight paths or manually piloting, an operator could simply tell the drone what goal to achieve. For example, "Drone, fly over to the east ridge and survey the area for any sign of wildfire" or "Find the red truck in the parking lot and circle it".

Human-Machine Collaboration

Drones will operate as part of a seamless human-machine team, enhancing safety and productivity in various environments. Rather than replacing humans, these AI drones will collaborate with people and other machines to accomplish goals more efficiently.
